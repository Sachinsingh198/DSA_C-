Baisc Structure of the doubly linked list:
    [ptr to prev node][data][ptr to the next node]
           head        head        tail

Advantages of Doubly linked lists over singly linked lists
    1. Traversal can be done in both ways.
    2. insertion and deletion becomes more efficient.
Disadvantages of doubly linked lists:
    1. Extra space for previous pointer.
    
Implementation of a node in a doubly linked list:

        NULL <--> [] <--> [] <--> [] <--> NULL
                 head            tail
